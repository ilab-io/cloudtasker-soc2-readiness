# SOC 2 Security Trust Services Criteria (TSC) — Selected Controls

This document lists the SOC 2 Security TSC controls that CloudTasker is implementing as part of its readiness project. These controls form the foundation of the security posture for the CloudTasker SaaS platform.

---

## CC1 — Control Environment
- CC1.1: Demonstrates commitment to integrity and ethical values  
- CC1.2: Board of Directors exercises oversight  
- CC1.3: Establishes structure, authority, and responsibility  
- CC1.4: Attracts, develops, and retains competent individuals  
- CC1.5: Holds individuals accountable  

---

## CC2 — Communication & Information
- CC2.1: Uses relevant, quality information  
- CC2.2: Communicates internally  
- CC2.3: Communicates externally  

---

## CC3 — Risk Assessment
- CC3.1: Specifies suitable objectives  
- CC3.2: Identifies and analyzes risks  
- CC3.3: Assesses fraud risk  
- CC3.4: Identifies and analyzes significant changes  

---

## CC4 — Monitoring Activities
- CC4.1: Conducts ongoing and/or separate evaluations  
- CC4.2: Evaluates and communicates deficiencies  

---

## CC5 — Control Activities
- CC5.1: Selects and develops control activities  
- CC5.2: Selects and develops general controls over technology  
- CC5.3: Deploys control activities through policies and procedures  

---

## Logical & Physical Access Controls (CC6.x)
- CC6.1: Restricts logical access  
- CC6.2: Implements authentication mechanisms  
- CC6.3: Restricts access to authorized users  
- CC6.6: Protects against unauthorized changes  
- CC6.7: Restricts physical access  

---

## System Operations (CC7.x)
- CC7.1: Detects and monitors anomalies  
- CC7.2: Logs and monitors system activity  
- CC7.3: Responds to identified issues  
- CC7.4: Manages changes  

---

## Change Management (CC8.x)
- CC8.1: Manages changes using formal processes  
- CC8.2: Documents and approves changes  
- CC8.3: Tests and validates changes  

---

## Risk Mitigation (CC9.x)
- CC9.1: Identifies and assesses risks  
- CC9.2: Implements risk mitigation activities  

---

# Notes
This list represents the **Security TSC only**, which is the scope of CloudTasker’s SOC 2 readiness project.
